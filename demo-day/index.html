Meet our community

At Makers we equip you with the skills you need to become a professional software engineer. By Week 11 we’ve completely revolutionised your approach to learning and taught you the ability to master any software from scratch. Most teams choose an entirely new language or framework to build their final project. Our Open Day is a great opportunity to talk to staff and students about the steep learning curve you’ll experience here.
