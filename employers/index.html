What they learn

Learning to be a developer at Makers Academy is less about what you know and more about how you do it.

Attitude - Students learn how to solve problems for themselves, to find their own answers.

Behaviour - They're pair-programming 80% of the time, working in scrum teams on projects and writing clean, maintainable code.

Technology - Our curriculum is mainly Ruby and Javascript, but students often teach themselves all manner of other technologies.
